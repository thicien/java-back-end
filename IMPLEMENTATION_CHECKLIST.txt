  Solution:
    1. Verify URL is: http://localhost:8080/java-back-end/carMarketplace

    4. View Tomcat logs: C:\apache-tomcat-11.0.15\logs\catalina.out

    1. Ensure RegisterServlet was compiled correctly
    2. Check you rebuilt with mvn clean install
